What is an MSME Loan?

An MSME (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprise) Loan is a form of business financing tailored specifically for entrepreneurs, startups, and small to medium-sized businesses. Whether you need working capital or funds for expansion, our MSME loans offer:

  • Loan amounts up to ₹[X] crores
  • Fast approvals & disbursals.
  • Easy documentation
  • No collateral required (for select loans)

Key Features & Benefits

  • Loan Amounts: ₹50,000 to ₹5 Crores+
  • Tenure: 12 to 60 months (Flexible)
  • Interest Rates: Competitive & transparent
  • Collateral-Free Loans: Available under select schemes
  • Fast Disbursal: Within 48–72 hours post-approval
  • Custom Loan Plans: Based on your business cash flow

Who Can Apply?

We support a wide range of MSMEs including:

  • Manufacturers
  • Traders
  • Service providers
  • Startups
  • Retailers
  • Wholesalers
  • Exporters & Importers

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Business should be registered under MSME (Udyam Registration optional but recommended)
  • Minimum 1 year of operational history (startups may vary)
  • Annual turnover of ₹[X] lakhs or more
  • Good credit history & bank statement record

Required Documents

  • PAN & Aadhaar (Proprietor/Partners/Directors)
  • Business registration documents (GST, Udyam, etc.)
  • Bank statements (last 6–12 months)
  • Income tax returns (last 1–2 years)
  • Financial statements (P&L, balance sheet)
  • KYC of business and owner

Our goal is to make your loan process simple, quick, and transparent by finding the lowest interest rates from multiple banks and guiding you at every step.
