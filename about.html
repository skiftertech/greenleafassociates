About us

About Me

Hi, I’m Annadurai, the founder of GreenLeaf Associates.

I have 12 years of experience working with leading private sector banks like ICICI Bank and Axis Bank, where I learned everything about home loans, customer needs, and the best ways to get faster approvals.

In August 2018, I started GreenLeaf Associates to help people in Tenkasi and surrounding areas get the best home loan deals without any stress. My goal is to make your loan process simple, quick, and transparent by finding the lowest interest rates from multiple banks and guiding you at every step.

Over the years, I’ve successfully helped 500+ families fulfill their dream of owning a home. Whether you need a new home loan, balance transfer, top-up loan, or loan against property, I’m here to provide personalized advice and ensure a smooth approval process.

"Your dream home deserves the right loan — and I’m here to make it happen."
